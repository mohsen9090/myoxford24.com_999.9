Interests — علایق

Patterns

I am interested in V‑ing
I am keen on V‑ing
I am enthusiastic about V‑ing
I am passionate about V‑ing
I am eager for V‑ing
I like / I enjoy V‑ing
I prefer V‑ing / to + V
I would rather + V

Examples

I am interested in reading.
I am passionate about photography.
I prefer walking to driving.

Skills & Abilities — مهارت‌ها

Patterns

I am skillful at V‑ing
I am good at V‑ing
I am capable of V‑ing
I am talented in V‑ing
I have expertise in V‑ing
I am able to + V
I can + V

Examples

I am good at problem‑solving.
I have expertise in marketing.
I am able to lead teams.
