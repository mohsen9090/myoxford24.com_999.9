Rules, Permission & Passive

Allowed / Not Allowed

I am/was/will be allowed to …
Smoking is banned / forbidden / prohibited.
It is against the law/rule to park here. • It is illegal / legal.

Let / Allow / Permit

I allow/permit you to go home early. • I let you go home early.
You are allowed/permitted to go home early. (You are let go home early ❌)

Optional vs Mandatory

It is optional / voluntary / not compulsory • It is compulsory/obligatory/mandatory • Insurance is mandatory

Passive voice — مجهول

am/is/are/was/were + past participle
The house was cleaned. • I am invited. • I am helped.

Insist / Obey

I insist that I should learn English. • I insist on learning English.
Obey the rules • Obedient / Disobedient • Stubborn • Rebellious
